Navigate to `System` -> `Package Manager` -> `Available Packages`

Search for `traffic totals` and click <kbd>➕Install</kbd>

![traffic-install](./img/traffic-install.png)

Click <kbd>✔️Confirm</kbd>

Wait until you see `Success`
